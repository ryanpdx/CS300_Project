Wining the Game:

To win the game collect all the Royal Jewels.

General:

Hero

The player's alter ego with the goal to collect all the Royal Jewels.

Whiffles

In game currency.

Movement

Use the arrow keys to move. Every attept at moving cost energy.

Terrain:

Grass

Cost 1 energy to move.

Bog

Cost 2 energy to move.

Grass

Requires a boat to travel on water.

Fog

Hides other terrain the hero has not seen.

Energy:

If you run out of energy the game ends.

Obstacles:

Trees

Trees cost energy to cut down unless the hero has an axe.

Boulder

Boulders cost energy to break unless the hero has an jackhammer.

Blackberry Bushes

Blackberry bushes cost energy to remove unless the hero has an weed wacker.

Tools:

Axe

Use to cut down trees without using energy.

Weed Wacker

Use to remove blackberry bushes without using energy.

Pickaxe

Use to remove boulders without using energy.

Binoculars

Allow the hero to see farther.

Items:

Royal Jewels

The hero will win the game if these are found.

Treasure Chest

Can be opened to get Whiffles.

Power Bars

Can be used to restore energy.
