General:

Winning the Game

To win the game, the hero must collect all the Royal Jewels.

Hero

The player's alter ego with the goal to collect all the Royal Jewels.

Whiffles (Wh)

In game currency and can be use to buy tools/items.

By default hero start with 100Wh.

Grovnick

One square tile of the island that make up the map.

Movement

Use the arrow keys to move one grovnick.

If the hero "jumps" of the edge of the island, they are teleported to opposite side of the island.

Every attept at moving cost energy.

Energy:

Moving and removing obstacles requres energy.

If you run out of energy the game ends.

By default hero start with 100 energy.

Terrain:

Sand

Cost 1 energy to move.

Bog

Cost 2 energy to move.

Water

Requires a boat to travel on water.

Fog

Hero can only see 1 grovnick in all directions.

If the hero has binoculars they can see 2 grovnick is all directions.

Hides other terrain the hero has not seen.

Obstacles:

Trees

Trees cost 10 energy to cut down without an axe and 3 energy with an axe.

Boulder

Boulders cost 15 energy to break without an Pickaxe and 10 with an Pickaxe.

Blackberry Bushes

Blackberry bushes cost 4 energy to remove without a Weed Wacker, 1 energy with a Weed Wacker.

Tools:

Axe

Reduces blackberry bushes removal cost by 7 energy.

Cost 10Wh, if found.

Weed Wacker

Reduces blackberry bushes removal cost by 5 energy.

Cost 10Wh, if found.

Pickaxe

Reduces boulders removal cost by 3 energy.

Cost 10Wh, if found.

Binoculars

Allow the hero to 2 grovnick is all direction.

Cost 50Wh, if found.

Items:

Royal Jewels

The hero will win the game if these are found.

Treasure Chests

Treasure chest contains 100 Whiffles.

Mimic Treasure Chests

Mimic treasure chests will take all of the hero's Whiffles.

WARNING!! Mimic treasure chests look the same as normal treasure chests.

Power Bars

Can be used to restore 20 energy.

Cost 1 Wh each.
